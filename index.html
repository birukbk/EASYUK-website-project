about

We are a group of young Ethiopians who got together and established EASYUK to cater for the needs of Ethiopian youths and students in the UK.​​​ The group started meeting together and began to organise ourselves to find solutions to the problems that face us within the UK and beyond.​ We started our journey by creating a yahoo mailing group and EASYUK was born. Initially the mailing group was a forum with which we could pass on information, news, current affairs as well as jokes.

After its establishment the organisation sought to achieve its goals through holding events, networking meetings, disseminating information, implementing themed and focussed projects.

EASYUK is a non-political and non-religious organisation and aims to be the means from which its members can connect with Ethiopia.
